About me

I am currently working as a Data Scientist at OpenX, an advertising technology company located in Pasadena, CA, where I have seen several projects from prototype to production. Most of my time is spent developing and validating machine learning models, defining performance metrics, and designing experiments and A/B tests. I also do data analysis and data visualization. My weapons of choice are Python, SQL, and Java, and I have experience working within a MapReduce framework, using Hadoop and Hive.

Prior to joining OpenX, I worked as a Graduate Research Assistant with the McEuen group at Cornell University, receiving my Ph.D. in Experimental Condensed Matter Physics in August 2015. As an experimentalist, I have an inquisitive nature, a passion for problem solving, and excellent debugging skills. I also have excellent communication and inter-personal skills, as exemplified by several collaborative projects in both industry and academia.

In my free time, I play video games, board games, card games, and role play games.

I am always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to learn new skills. If you could use a problem solver with a unique perspective, feel free to reach out.
